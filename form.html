Rules & Regulations

  1. Open to all college students. Teams can consist of 4 to 6 members.
  2. All the team members should be from the same college.
  3. No team leader or member(s) can be in more than one team.
  4. All participants must register online before the deadline. Late entries will not be accepted.
  5. Participants can use any materials except for pre-made kits or fully assembled models. All materials must be sourced independently.
  6. All models must be original creations. Plagiarism or copying from existing designs will lead to disqualification.
  7. Teams must prepare a brief presentation (maximum 5 minutes) to explain their model's functionality and design process.
  8. Participants must follow safety guidelines when working with tools and materials. The organizing committee will not be responsible for any injuries.
  9. Models will be evaluated based on creativity, functionality, aesthetics, and presentation. Detailed criteria will be provided.
  10. Any behavior deemed unsportsmanlike or violations of the rules may result in disqualification.
  11. Teams must prepare a brief presentation (maximum 5 minutes) to explain their model's functionality and design process.
  12. The Project should be either fully Hardware or Hardware-Software. No fully Software Projects are allowed.
  13. Trophies and Cash Prizes will be awarded for Top 10 teams which also consist of Top 3 and other 5 special prizes.
  14. Accommodation will not be provided by Us. For accommodation Contact Us.
